# AcountPay Merchant POS Platform

Accept bank account payments via QR codes with our easy-to-use Point of Sale system. Perfect for restaurants, retail stores, service businesses, and any merchant wanting to offer modern digital payments.

## Why Choose AcountPay POS?

- **Lower Fees**: Bank-to-bank payments cost less than card processing
- **Instant Setup**: Start accepting payments in minutes
- **No Hardware**: Works on any tablet, phone, or computer
- **Secure**: Bank-grade security with no card data to protect
- **Customer Friendly**: QR codes work with any banking app

## How It Works

1. **Enter Payment Details**: Amount and customer's bank
2. **Generate QR Code**: System creates secure payment code
3. **Customer Pays**: They scan and authorize through their banking app
4. **Instant Confirmation**: Both parties receive immediate confirmation
